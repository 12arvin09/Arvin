Just as a fire ignites from a small ember, my long-held dream of contributing to the technology field was truly sparked by a simple coding experience during my ONDOSpace internship. Throughout high school, my combined knowledge of computer science and physics remained largely theoretical and lacked practical application. However, at ONDOSpace, I suddenly felt a that amazing magic like skill through programming, and I passionately decided to create everything possible with the knowledge I had gained. Motivated by my desire to help people with disabilities and powered by my growing intellectual curiosity, my technology journey began. It ranged from Brain Computer Interfaces to the development of smart wearable devices. Lastly, I am deeply grateful for the support of the engineer brothers who guided me through every challenge and generously allowed me to use their advanced equipment and gadgets.
