After spending six incredible months at this amazing place, I'm finally beginning to feel the impact of my second gap year. People often say, "Hurry up and learn." But I’ve come to realize something deeper. Rather than pretending to be someone who never wastes time but fills their head with knowledge they don’t truly understand, I want to become someone who is consciously shaping their future—someone who overcomes challenges, finds joy in the process, and uses the opportunities available to young people with clarity and purpose. On this journey, I found ONDOSPACE. Before this, the word "engineer" was just a title to me—something that sounded powerful but meant little beyond that. I didn’t know what engineers actually did, how they built things, or why they did it. But since joining ONDOSPACE on December 12th, I've started to move away from pretending to know everything and toward truly filling in the gaps of what I don’t know. I’m deeply grateful to the mentors—those older brothers and sisters—who welcomed me without judgment. You never labeled me as “empty.” Instead, you reflected on even my smallest actions with care, and gave me encouragement, knowledge, and inspiration that I will carry forward. With everything I’ve gained here, I’m stepping into the university I’ve been working toward—not just with ambition, but with purpose. And I’ll carry the spirit you gave me wherever I go.
