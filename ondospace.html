After spending six incredible months at this amazing place, I have finally felt the impact of my second gap year. People often say, "Hurry up and learn." But I have come to realize something deeper. Rather than pretending to be someone who never wastes time but fills their head with knowledge they don’t truly want or don’t even know what to do with in the future, I want to become someone who consciously tries to contribute to the future of us all : someone who chooses challenging paths, finds joy in the process even in failure, and uses the opportunities available to young people with clarity and purpose. And through this journey, I found this amazing place: ONDOSPACE. Before entering this place, the word "engineer" was just a title to me, something that sounded powerful but meant little beyond that. I didn’t know what engineers actually did, how they built things, or why they did it. But since joining ONDOSPACE on June 12th, I've started to move away from pretending to know everything and toward truly filling in the gaps of what I don’t know. I’m deeply grateful to the mentors 'those older brothers and sisters' who welcomed me without judgment. They never labeled me as “empty.” Instead, they reflected on even my smallest actions with care and gave me encouragement, knowledge, and inspiration that I will carry forward. With everything I’ve gained here, I’m stepping into my next path university not just with ambition, but with purpose. And I’ll carry the spirit that ONDOSPACE gave me wherever I go.
